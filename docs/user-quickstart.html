CardTracker

Your AI-powered credit card payoff planner. From setup to debt-free in five steps.

Getting Started
1

Create Your Profile

Enter your name and choose an avatar color. Set a Recovery PIN — a memorable word or phrase like smithfamily. This PIN lets you recover your data on any device without an account.

☁ Cloud Sync 💾 Local Backup
2

Add Your Credit Cards

Tap + Add Card for each card. Enter balance, APR, credit limit, and monthly payment. Set the due day and statement close day to unlock the calendar and alerts.

Minimum required: Balance + APR
3

Add Your API Key

Tap 🔑 in the top bar and paste your Anthropic API key. This unlocks AI Analysis, Strategy Builder, and What-If chat for everyone who uses this artifact URL.

Key stored in shared cloud storage — set once, works for all users.
4

Get Your AI Strategy

Open Payoff Strategy Planner (📊 button). Answer 5 questions about your goals. Claude builds a custom plan and recommends Avalanche or Snowball. Tap Apply This Strategy.

5 Questions → AI Plan → Apply ✅
Payoff Methods — Which Should You Choose?

🔥 Avalanche — Best for saving money

Pay highest APR card first. Mathematically optimal — saves the most interest. Best if you can stay motivated without quick wins.

Highest APR first → lowest APR last

❄️ Snowball — Best for motivation

Pay smallest balance first. Each payoff is a visible win. Costs slightly more interest but keeps you going when it's hard.

Smallest balance first → largest balance last
Claude picks the right one for your situation after the 5-question questionnaire.

💡 Pro Tips
💰

Use the Extra Budget field

Even $50/month extra can cut months off your payoff. Enter it in the Planner header — the schedule recalculates instantly.

🎯

Rerun strategy after big changes

Got a raise? Paid off a card? Change in goals? Open Strategy → Rerun Questionnaire → apply the fresh plan.

📋

Export before editing cards

Tap 📦 → Export JSON before making major changes. A 30-second backup beats a full re-entry.
