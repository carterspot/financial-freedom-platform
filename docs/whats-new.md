# CardTracker — What's New
**Version 3.0 · March 2026**

---

## 🎯 Smarter Payoff Strategy Planner

### Strategy Tab is Now First
The Payoff Strategy Planner now opens on the **Strategy tab** for first-time users, guiding you to build your personalized plan before looking at any numbers. Returning users land directly on the **Schedule tab** — your plan is already saved and waiting.

### AI Strategy Now Saved Automatically
Every strategy you generate is saved silently in the background. Reopen the Planner any time and your last analysis is right there with its date — no need to regenerate from scratch unless your situation has changed.

### Apply This Strategy Button
After Claude generates your plan, a new **Apply This Strategy** button appears. One tap sets the Planner to Claude's recommended method (Avalanche or Snowball), applies the suggested extra budget amount, and shows a confirmation banner so you always know which strategy is active. The Apply button is also available on the **Schedule tab** so you can switch strategies without hunting through tabs.

### Questionnaire Answers Saved
Your 5 answers to the strategy questionnaire are now remembered. When you return to rerun your strategy, your previous answers are pre-selected — just change what's different and regenerate.

### Rerun Strategy Anytime
A **Rerun Questionnaire** button on the Strategy tab lets you update your answers and generate a fresh plan whenever your goals, income, or situation changes. The previous result stays visible until you generate a new one.

## ✓ Quick Payment Logging

A new **✓ Pay** button on every card panel lets you log a payment in seconds without opening the full Planner.

- Pre-filled with your expected monthly payment
- Edit the actual amount — over or under payments both work
- Shows real-time breakdown: interest portion, principal portion, new balance preview
- On confirm, your card balance updates immediately — every chart, payoff date, and AI analysis reflects the real number going forward
- Date defaults to today but is editable for back-dating

---

## 💾 Export AI Results

Both AI features — Schedule Analysis and Strategy Builder — now have export options:

- **📋 Copy** — copies the full analysis to your clipboard instantly
- **⬇ Download .txt** — saves a self-contained text file including the date, your card snapshot, and the full analysis

The exported file is readable anywhere: Notes, Google Drive, email, or a shared folder.

## 📱 Responsive Design (All Screen Sizes)

The app now works properly on phones and tablets:

- **Mobile** — single-column card grid, compact nav bar (avatar only, shortened buttons), smaller calendar cells, abbreviated day headers
- **Tablet** — two-column card grid, two-column summary stats
- **Desktop** — unchanged

## 📅 Calendar Export (.ics)

Export your payment calendar to any calendar app with a single tap:

- Monthly recurring **payment due** events for every card
- Monthly recurring **statement close** events
- **3-day and 1-day reminder alarms** on every payment due event
- **One-time payoff date** event when projected payoff is calculated
- Works with **Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook**, and any app that accepts `.ics` files
