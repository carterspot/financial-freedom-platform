⚡ What makes DebtTracker different

DebtTracker combines CardTracker and LoanTracker into one unified planner. Your avalanche or snowball strategy runs across all your debt simultaneously — credit cards and loans compete for your extra dollars together, not separately.

💳 Credit Cards 🏦 Auto Loans 🎓 Student Loans 🏠 Mortgage 💼 Personal Loans
Getting Started
1

Create Your Profile

Enter your name and set a Recovery PIN — a memorable word like smithfamily. This syncs your data across every device and every FFP module. One PIN, one family.

☁ Cloud Sync 💾 Local Backup
2

Add Your Debts

Tap + Add Card or + Add Loan. Cards need balance, APR, limit, and due date. Loans need balance, rate, and term. Mix and match freely.

Already use CardTracker or LoanTracker? Your data imports via JSON backup.
3

Add Your API Key

Tap 🔑 in the top bar. Paste your Anthropic API key once — it's shared across all FFP modules automatically. This unlocks AI Analysis, Strategy Builder, and What-If chat.

Already set it in CardTracker or LoanTracker? It's already here.
4

Run the Unified Planner

Open the Payoff Planner (📊 button). Choose Avalanche or Snowball — the engine optimizes across cards AND loans together. Add extra budget to see months shaved off your payoff.

All Debts → One Plan → Debt Free ✅
How the Unified Planner Works

🔥 Avalanche — Save the most money

Ranks ALL debts — cards and loans — by APR. Your 24% credit card beats your 7% auto loan. Extra dollars always go to the highest-rate debt regardless of type.

Highest APR first across all debt types

❄️ Snowball — Stay motivated

Ranks ALL debts by balance. That $800 medical bill gets crushed first even if your student loan rate is higher. Quick wins across any debt type.

Smallest balance first across all debt types
Use the grouping toggle to view debts by type (cards then loans) or interleaved by APR.

💡 Pro Tips
⚡

Use the grouping toggle

Default view groups cards then loans. Switch to interleaved-by-APR to see your true payoff priority across all debt types.

💰

Extra budget is the multiplier

Even $100/month extra across a mixed debt portfolio can eliminate years. Try it in the Schedule tab — the math is eye-opening.

🔄

Check Refinance AI on loans

If rates have dropped since you took a loan, run the Refinance AI tab. It shows exact savings and whether closing costs are worth it.
